There are many things that you can do in the 100 Acre Woods. This includes spending a day with Christopher Robin and Pooh, reading with Owl, gardening with Rabbit, having tea at Piglet's, talking with Eeyore, having dinner with Kanga and Roo, and excersizing with Tigger.
